---
title: "Writing in public"
description: "Why short posts beat perfect drafts."
date: "May 7 2026"
---

Publishing small notes forces you to finish a thought instead of letting it live forever in a notes app. The goal is not a polished essay every time; it is a trail someone else (or future you) can follow.

Start with one concrete observation, one link, or one snippet. Ship it, then refine or follow up if the idea keeps pulling you back.
